FOIA and Archiving in the Digital Age

 

Presenters

Reed Duecy-Gibbs
NextRequest
 

Ira Dixon
PageFreezer
 

 

Description

Are you clear on the requirements for – and differences between – open records, archiving, FOIA, data authenticity and data integrity when it comes to government web, social media and information requests? Managing FOIA and records requests is becoming increasingly time-consuming and litigious. This session will review best practices to save government staff time, make legal compliance easier, and provide a better more transparent experience for the public.

Topics:

  • Differences between Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) and Open Records requests
  • Recent trends in records request laws
  • Specificity (how to get requesters to narrow and define their requests better) and business processes best practices
  • Requirements and options for website and social media archiving
  • Understanding data authenticity and data integrity
  • Highlights of archiving vs back-up
  • Costs of non-compliance with open records laws
